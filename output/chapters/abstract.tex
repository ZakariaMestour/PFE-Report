\chapter*{R\'esum\'e}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{R\'esum\'e}

This report presents the end-of-studies internship carried out at Adria Business \& Technology within the Supply Chain Finance Platform Team. The work took place in an Agile delivery environment where Jira tickets, Bitbucket pull requests, technical review, and tester validation structured the development process. The report first situates the project in its organizational and banking context, then studies the architecture of the platform, its core SCF domain model, and the main frontend flows exposed to bank, buyer, and supplier users.

The academic project focuses on the Disbursement Module, a functional block responsible for executing financing after invoice eligibility checks and program rules are satisfied. The module was refined with the Product Owner through weekly discussions and diagrams, leading to a clearer model involving programs, invoices, disbursements, finance records, transactions, manual requests, automatic disbursement, and asynchronous payment callbacks. The internship also strengthened practical skills in Spring Boot, React, TypeScript, Keycloak-based security, gateway routing, JPA modeling, and collaborative software delivery.

\textbf{Keywords:} Supply Chain Finance, Disbursement, Spring Boot, React, Keycloak, Jira, Banking Platform.
